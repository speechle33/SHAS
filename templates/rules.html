Game Rules

Blackjack is a card game where the goal is to score more points than the dealer without exceeding 21 points.

Ground rules::

  • The game uses a standard deck of 52 cards.
  • The player receives two cards.
  • The player scores points based on the values of the cards:
    • Cards 2 through 10 have a face value.
    • Pictures (Jacks, Ladies, Kings) are worth 10 points.
    • Aces are worth 11 points if the sum of the cards in your hand is 21 points when you receive an Ace. Otherwise, an Ace is worth 1.
  • The player can take additional cards (“Hit”) or stop (“Stand”).
  • If a player's point total exceeds 21, the player loses.
  • After the player completes his turn, the dealer reveals his cards and fills until he has at least 17 points.
  • A player wins if his point total is higher than the dealer's point total.
  • If the sums of the player's and dealer's points are equal, the player loses.

Good luck in the game and may luck be with you!
